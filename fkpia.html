Guide for Prospective Eventers

  1. Get one of the SIMs and activate it. If you will be in Japan before you need the account, you can get one the SIMs that require activation in Japan. Otherwise, you only choice is to get the Rakuten SIM shipped to your home country.
  2. Sign up for one of the VoIP service.
  3. Sign up to Pia and e+ through the VoIP service.

Main differences between the SIM providers

  • Mobal: The only provider that has eSIMs (Rakuten might have eSIMs now).
  • Hanacell: Cheapest to park your number (8 USD/yr).
  • Rakuten Pre-paid: The only provider that allows you to activate and receive SMS overseas. Cannot receive calls.

None of these providers allow calling from overseas. You must sign up for a VoIP service like Viber or TalkU for that.

2024-10 Update: Rakuten now offers an eSIM for data+SMS plans. It has not been tested yet.
